Helpful Hints & Tips

  • Only businesses that are LISTED can be searched here. If you are searching for an unlisted business, you must know the exact name. We do an exact match search on unlisted businesses.
  • Joining a business, sends a request to the Business Managers of this business. Either the Business Master or the elected managers can approve a join request. Upon approval, the join is successful.
